About me

My name is Vlasta Nosova. I'm 15 and I'm from Uzhhorod, Ukraine. I study at Uzhgorod Specialized School №3 with extensive English learning. My birthday is on the 5th of September.

I always get good marks in the school, because I'm an excellent student. But, actually, I don't enjoy studying, especially the subjects I don't like. These include Physics, History and Russian Literature. The school subjects I like are Chemistry, English and Math. I like speaking English,because it seems so easy for me. I also love Math, because it's the subject I can understand without any help, and I like Chemistry, because I enjoy going to my tutor.

I don't have many hobbies, but I believe I'm skillful at many things. For example, I don't draw a lot, but I can draw a person very accurately. I also like watching TV series. These days I'm watching "Supernatural". The thing I enjoy the most in my life is dancing and listening to music. I want to become a choreographer in the future. I just wish I could go to some dance classes, but as my town is really small, there aren't any good ones.
